About

My Story

Where are you based?

I was born and raised in the beautiful and vibrant city of Calgary. I have dual citizenship with Canada and Australia, and have been fortunate enough to have been able to live and work in both countries. I currently reside in Calgary.

How do you spend your free time?

In my free time I love to workout and keep fit. Boxing and kickboxing are my two exercises of passion. Training hard and staying active give my life discipline and keep my mind sharp. I love the outdoors!! I love living in Alberta because we have the glorious and magnificent mountains. I love hiking and being out in nature. I enjoy cartooning and doing illustrations. I have published art in one children's book so far and am currently working on art for another childrens story.

Music is my biggest passion. I have been singing since I was a child, and have been fortunate to have performed all over the world with very talented music ensembles. I am currently working on my own music and performing and open mics.

What’s a fun fact about you?

A fun fact about me is that I lived in a rainforest for 6 months in Northern Queensland in and around the Daintree Rainforest. I have never seen bugs so big haha!

What’s your professional experience?

I started my post-secondary education by pursuing a business degree. I fell in love with marketing and pursued a double major in marketing and management. While studying marketing I fell in love with a graphic design course I took. The artist in me loved to be able to express ideas through words, lines and shapes, so after I finished my degree I pursued schooling in graphic design and now I am actively pursuing education in UX Design.

I have been in school for 8 years and while pursuing my education I have worked in various industries. My first job was at Apple technologies where I started as a specialist selling and educating customers on Apple products. From there I went full force into the retail world where I have worked as a sales associate, merchandising manager, marketing coordinator to an e-commerce coordinator.

I have also worked in the travel and hospitality industry in the events management department. I have always worked directly with customers, people are my passion, and I want to always be in positions where I am always working with customers and clients.

After completing my graphic design course, I started freelancing under JS Designs. Most of my design work has been for local start-up companies that need business and marketing advice as well as design work in terms of logo development, front-end web design, marketing collateral)

I am very excited for the next chapter of my career. I am looking to align myself with a small company that focuses on not-for-profit work or in the start-up and development of new local businesses.
