Olive Mobile App

Building a better you from the inside out

Overview:

Olive is a health and wellness app aimed at providing a holistic approach to health and fitness

Purpose:

Olive was designed to be fun and interactive, allowing health conscious individuals to track and build health daily habits to better improve their overall health

Role:

UX and UI Designer UX Researcher

Duration:

January 2021-June 2021

Tools

Adobe XD, Adobe Illustrator

Research Process

Through user research and interviews I was able to create 3 user personas that reflected the idea user for O-Live. From there I created 3 main features that would allow users the ability to add food, modify medical information and search information on the web.

Ideation

After conducting user testing I was able to make improvements to my app, looking at pain points and looking at areas that worked and using those techniques to improve the app.
